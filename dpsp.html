Our dPSP Solution

  • Lyceum’s Digital Patient Support (dPSP) is a web-based application which supports patients as they start and continue on therapy.
  • Providing support to navigate financial reimbursement as well as psychosocial support, Lyceum’s dPSP meets patients where they are to ensure they are empowered and engaged throughout their treatment journey.
  • Lyceum’s dPSP complements the care provided by the patient’s primary care team and saves time by providing insights related to possible psychosocial barriers to treatment.
  • Using technology to scale and support patients 24/7, Lyceum’s dPSP is a cost-effective and efficient way to reach and engage patients when compared with traditional call-centres.
