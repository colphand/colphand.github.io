About Me

Hi! I am a French Product Designer currently based in Toronto.

Currently Product Lead @ Lyceum Health.

I consider myself a generalist designer, well versed in product strategy and development. I thrive in startups, scaleups and more generally small and fast paced environments.

I’ve worked with startups, SaaS companies, agencies and as a freelancer. I’ve been one of a team and a team of one.

I’ve designed products, systems, and processes. I’ve led design teams and I mentor at Designlab and MyJobGlasses.

I love building digital products. It is a field so complex and so rewarding. Everything boils down to why, what and how to build. Strategy, Design and Code. I consider myself a generalist, and I thrive in startups and scaleups. I love to see the bigger picture, and then dive way down to Design Tokens.

I embrace the messiness of processes, debates, analytics, and technical debt. I learned front-end code. I am currently open for work. Looking for FT and freelance gigs. Shoot me an email!

Even more about me!

I like when things move fast. I love to collaborate. You know that feeling of flow, but with collaboratively? That one that stems from a great meeting, solving a well defined problem, by committed, skilled people? That’s my jam. I love to give back to the community: I am currently mentoring at Designlab and MyJobGlasses.
