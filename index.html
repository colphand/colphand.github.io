Our Solutions

Clinical Assessment Programs™ (CAP)

Lyceum Health’s Clinical Assessment programs reduce administration and improve visibility to patient experience when starting and switching therapies.

With real time insights from assessments, distribution of patient education and clinical instructions our CAPs are utilized to help busy providers provide efficient and effective care to optimize treatments

PrompTx™

In the world of patient support programs, the PrompTx™ web application provides a behavioural science-backed approach to patient communication.

Proprietary algorithms informed by the latest research in behavioural support segment patient needs so the right patient support is provided to the right patient at the right time.

Patient support programs incorporate PrompTx to uncover and address barriers to non-starts and discontinuation while reporting progress to providers – delivering complementary support to busy clinics, new patient insights and tangible ROI.

Our Clients

For Physicians

CAP™ and PrompTx™ are private and secure ways for clinics to:

  • Improve continuity of support and reduce burdensome administration when starting and switching patient therapy and manufacturer-sponsored patient support programs
  • Increase visibility on what and how patients are doing by providing clinical information for patient consults
  • Increase access for patients while creating a centralized way to distribute patient education / instruction materials
  • Receive periodic reports on patient population for practice reflection and patient barriers to therapy
  • Leverage technology to meet the increasing need to support patients remotely between clinic visits

For Manufacturers

CAP™ and PrompTx™ are IMC compliant ways to:

  • Generate new insights and data continuity
  • Deliver measurable and proven program ROI to accomplish objectives
  • Help providers optimize treatments while complementing clinics to assist with patient monitoring and support
  • Generate incremental connections with busy providers utilizing meaningful data and presentations
  • Reduce provider burden of starting and switching patient therapy
