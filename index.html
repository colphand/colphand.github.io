Our Solutions

At Lyceum Health, we support specialty care patients by getting them onto treatment and by ensuring they get the most from therapy along the way.
We do this by connecting doctors, patients and the life science industry with our proprietary, data driven applications.

Clinical Assessment Programs (CAP)

Lyceum’s CAP helps specialty pharmaceutical manufacturers support specialists to start patients on treatment faster by removing administrative burdens.


  • Identify patients ready and eligible for specialty therapies
  • Data collection for prior authorizations
  • Easily identify treatment gaps
  • Automated reporting

Digital Patient Support Programs (dPSP)

Lyceum’s patient support programs use proprietary technology to improve the physician and patient experience while reducing costs versus to traditional PSPs.


  • Improved patient enrollment and engagement
  • Automated reimbursement
  • Automated follow up and adherence check-ins
  • Reduced AE reporting
Learn More

Digital Therapeutics (DTx)

Lyceum’s first DTx – iCALM – is the world’s first digital psychotherapeutic that uses researched methods to reduce depression and anxiety for patients and caregivers facing advance cancer.


  • Developed in partnership with Princess Margaret Cancer Center, University Health Network
  • Improves accessibility and cost-effectiveness of mental health for employees and caregivers
  • Reduces depression, anxiety, and other mental health concerns that can lead to absenteeism and disability
